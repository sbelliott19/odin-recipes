Buttermilk Glaze

Description

This buttermilk glaze is one of the crucial components to making the Best Carrot Cake live up to it's name!

This glaze can also be used on other cakes to "level-up" the flavor!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1-1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk
  • 1/2 cup butter or margarine
  • 1 tablespoon light corn syrup
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Bring first 5 ingredients to a boil in a large Dutch oven over medium-high heat.
  2. Boil, stirring often, 4 minutes.
  3. Remove from heat, and stir in vanilla
