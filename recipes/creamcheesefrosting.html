Cream Cheese Frosting

Description

This is the cream cheese frosting for the Best Carrot Cake.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter or margarine, softened
  • 11 ounces cream cheese, softened (about a block and a half)
  • 1 (16-ounce) package powdered sugar
  • 1-1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Beat butter and cream cheese at medium speed with an electric mixer until creamy.
  2. Add powdered sugar and vanilla extract; beat at high speed 10 seconds or until smooth.
